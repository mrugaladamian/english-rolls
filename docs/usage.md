# English Rolls User Guide

English Rolls is a simple app for Polish speakers who want to practice basic English words and phrases.

## Practicing Vocabulary

After opening the app, a Polish word or phrase appears on the screen.

1. Read the Polish version.
2. Click the screen to reveal the English translation.
3. Check the pronunciation shown with the English version.
4. Click the screen again to move to the next item.

## Marking Known Words

When the English version is visible, you can click the `✓` button.

This marks the word as completed, so it should not return in the same practice session.

## Word Mastery System

A word is considered **mastered** when:

- It is marked as completed **on the first attempt** in a session, **or**
- It is marked as completed **three times in a row** if it was not completed on the first attempt.

## Pronunciation

The speaker icon turns English pronunciation on or off.

- When the icon is fully visible, pronunciation is enabled.
- When the icon is faded, pronunciation is disabled.

## Background Video

The film icon lets you choose a custom video background.

After clicking the icon, select a video file from your computer. The selected video will play in the background while you practice.

## Progress Counter

The number visible on the screen shows how many items are left in the current session.

## Typical Workflow

1. Open the app.
2. Enable pronunciation if you want to practice listening.
3. Read the Polish word or phrase.
4. Click the screen and check the English version.
5. Repeat the English word out loud.
6. Click `✓` if you already know the word.
7. Move to the next item.
